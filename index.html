Deathblade Overview

The Deathblade in Lost Ark is an Assassin type class that wields 3 Swords; two Daggers and one Longsword. The Deathblade is a Melee DPS, primarily attacking in close rage distance. They fight with the flurry of a thousand cuts with a chaotic fast-paced slashing style. Their musical instrument is a Flute.

The Identity Gauge of the Deathblade is the Art Meter. The Art Meter fills upon successful hits with attacks filling up 3 Death Orbs. Death Orbs can be activated with z and the Deathblade will enter into an enhanced state known as Art. Art gives the player a number of beneficial improvements: granting immunity to crowd control effects, restoring Mana to full, and lastly, depending on how many orbs were activated will provide greater benefits. The scaling benefit is as follows:

Activated Death Orbs Duration Movement speed Attack Speed Attack Power Cooldown Reduction Mana Regeneration
1 10 seconds + 10% + 10% + 10% 10% faster 15% faster
2 20 seconds + 10% + 15% + 20% 30% faster 25% faster
3 30 seconds + 10% + 20% + 30% 50% faster 45% faster

Lastly, while in Art, the Deathblade can activate the Identity Gauge a second time causing a damaging ability: Deathblade Surge Z. This knocks down opponents and deals damage based on the number of Orbs used. Once this second activation is executed the Deathblade will exit this Art state.
