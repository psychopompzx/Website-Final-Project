BUILDS

To maximize damage while leveling, you should focus all your skill points on the following skills:

  • Blitz Rush
  • Moonlight Sonic
  • Turning Slash
  • Earth Cleaver
  • Void Strike
  • Soul Absorber

EXTRA TIPS:

  • Engage with skills such as Blitz Rush or Spin Cutter, then follow up with swing skills to launch your enemies in the air before pulling off any charge skill like Void Strike.
  • The dash skills can also be saved and used to escape incoming attacks.

PVP

For a Deathblade in PvP it focuses more on skills that provide mobility for you, but inflict immobility on your enemy as you try and outdance each other in the arena.
